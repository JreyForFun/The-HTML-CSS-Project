About The WebiDev Project

Empowering the next generation of Web Enginners.

Our Mission

The WebiDev is dedicated to providing a free, open-source, and comprehensive curriculum for learning Web Development. We believe in accessible education for all, with a focus on hands-on projects and real-world applications.

Our Vision

To build a global community of learners and contributors passionate about advancing the field of Web Development.
